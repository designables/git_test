What's a Good FiOS Speed?

The ideal FiOS speed depends on your household's internet needs. Here's a general guideline:

  • 1-5 Mbps: Basic web browsing and email
  • 5-10 Mbps: Streaming SD videos and music
  • 10-25 Mbps: Streaming HD videos and online gaming
  • 25+ Mbps: Multiple devices streaming, downloading large files, and online gaming

Tips for Optimizing Your FiOS Speed

To get the most out of your FiOS speed test, follow these tips:

  • If testing on PC, connect computer to the router directly with Ethernet cable for practical speed test.
  • Place your router in central location at home for optimal coverage of signals.
  • It is suggested to avoid placing your router inside box/container, near walls or other objects that can interfere with the signal.
  • Restart your router a few times to diagnose potential issues.

If you're consistently getting slow speeds, contact Verizon FiOS customer support for assistance.
